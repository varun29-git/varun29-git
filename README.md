## About

Undergraduate student studying **Computer Science and Physics**.

I spend most of my time exploring how intelligent systems work by building models and studying the mathematics behind them. My interests sit at the intersection of **artificial intelligence, computation, and physics**, with curiosity about how future systems may combine ideas from machine learning, quantum computing, and physical reasoning.

**Artificial Intelligence**  
Design and behavior of modern AI systems, especially language models and efficient architectures.

**Computation and Efficiency**  
Understanding how intelligent systems can operate with less compute, memory, and energy.

**Physical and Spatial Intelligence**  
Exploring how models can represent and reason about the structure of the physical world.

**Quantum Computing**  
Learning the foundations of quantum information and its relationship to computation. Focus on Quantum Search Algorithms.
